Shipping Policy

Thank you for shopping with SuitFlick. We are committed to delivering your order safely and as quickly as possible.

Order Processing

Orders are processed after successful payment confirmation. Processing usually takes 1–2 business days.

Delivery Time

Estimated delivery time is generally 3–7 business days depending on your location and courier service availability.

Shipping Coverage

We currently deliver to most locations across India. Delivery availability may vary for some remote areas.

Shipping Charges

Free shipping is available on eligible orders as displayed on the website. Shipping charges, if applicable, will be shown during checkout.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will be informed through the contact details you provided during checkout.

Delivery Delays

Unexpected delays may occur due to weather conditions, public holidays, courier issues, or other circumstances beyond our control.

Incorrect Address

Please make sure your delivery address and mobile number are correct while placing your order. SuitFlick is not responsible for delays caused by incorrect information provided by the customer.

Need Help?

If you have any questions regarding your shipment, please contact our customer support through the Contact page.
