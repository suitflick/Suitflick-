Return & Refund Policy

At SuitFlick, customer satisfaction is important to us. Please read our Return & Refund Policy carefully before placing an order.

Return Eligibility

Products can be returned only if they are received in a damaged, defective, or incorrect condition.

To request a return, please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order and provide clear photos of the product.

Items Not Eligible for Return

  • Products used or washed by the customer.
  • Products without original packaging or tags.
  • Products damaged after delivery due to customer handling.
  • Customized or special-order products.

Refund Process

Once your returned product is received and inspected, we will notify you about the approval or rejection of your refund request.

If approved, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days using the original payment method or another mutually agreed method.

Exchange Policy

If you receive the wrong size, wrong product, or a damaged item, we will arrange a replacement whenever possible, subject to product availability.

Need Help?

For any return or refund-related questions, please contact our customer support through the Contact page.
