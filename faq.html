Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

Browse products, add your favorite items to the cart, proceed to checkout, complete the payment using the UPI QR Code, and place your order.

2. Which payment methods are accepted?

Currently, payments are accepted through the UPI QR Code displayed during checkout.

3. How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 3–7 business days depending on your delivery location.

4. Can I cancel my order?

Please contact our customer support as soon as possible. Cancellation is only possible before the order has been shipped.

5. Can I return a product?

Yes. Returns are accepted only for damaged, defective, or incorrect products according to our Return & Refund Policy.

6. How can I contact SuitFlick?

You can contact us through the Contact page, Email, or WhatsApp during our support hours.

7. Is my personal information safe?

Yes. We value your privacy and handle your information according to our Privacy Policy.

8. Do you deliver across India?

Yes. We deliver to most serviceable locations across India.
